About me

Hi, I’m Emery.

I like Language.

I’m a computational linguist studying at Northeastern University. I’m specifically interested in exploring programs that interact with natural languages. Computer Science and Linguistics interest me individually, as well. For the former, my interests mainly lie in automata and formal language theory. My Linguistics interests are expansive. Morphosyntax is my forte, but I have also pursued everything from phonological systems and psycholinguistics to sociolinguistics.

Lots of my time is spent studying languages. While I hesitate to describe myself as anything beyond monolingual, I’ve invested time into learning the following, in order of competence:

  • Latin

  • Hebrew

  • Finnish

  • Scottish Gaelic

  • Mandarin Chinese

  • Georgian

To the collective groan of thousands of conlangers, I’m an Esperantist, mostly out of respect for Zamenhof.
